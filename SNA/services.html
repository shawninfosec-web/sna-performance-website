What We Offer

Our Services

Everything your car needs — from stance setups and bolt-on power to full audio builds and exterior installs. If it makes your car better, we do it.

Category 01

Wheels, Tires & Suspension

Whether you're building a slammed show car or a precise track weapon, we have the expertise to get your suspension dialed in exactly how you want it.

📐

Stance Setups

Get the perfect fitment. We help you dial in wheel offset, ride height, and camber to achieve that aggressive stance without sacrificing driveability.

  • Wheel fitment consultation
  • Lowering spring & coilover installs
  • Camber kit installs
  • Flush & poke fitment
🏁

Street & Track Setup

Performance-oriented suspension tuning for drivers who want both street manners and track capability. We set your car up to handle.

  • Coilover setup & corner balancing
  • Sway bar upgrades
  • Alignment for track or street
  • Bushing & subframe upgrades
🔍

Handling Upgrades & Diagnostics

Clunks, vague steering, or uneven wear? We diagnose and fix suspension issues and recommend upgrades that will genuinely improve your driving experience.

  • Full suspension inspection
  • Ball joint & tie rod replacement
  • Strut & shock replacement
  • Steering & handling troubleshooting
🛞

Wheels & Tires

New set of wheels? We mount, balance, and set tire pressure properly. We can also advise on the best tire for your use case.

  • Wheel mounting & balancing
  • TPMS programming
  • Tire fitment advice
  • Spacer installs

Cold Air Intakes

Increase airflow and get a better sound under the hood. We install short ram and cold air intakes for most makes and models.

  • Intake spacers
  • Filter upgrades

Throttle Body Upgrades

A larger throttle body helps your engine breathe better at wide open throttle. We source and install the right throttle body for your setup.

Exhaust Headers & Manifolds

Long-tube or shorty headers — we handle the install. Proper header fitment can make a significant difference in power and exhaust tone.

  • H-pipe & X-pipe installs

Cat-Back Exhaust Systems

Get the sound and performance you want. We install axle-back and cat-back exhaust systems with proper fitment and minimal drone.

  • Cat-back system installs
  • Axle-back installs
  • Muffler replacements
  • Active exhaust systems
