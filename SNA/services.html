What We Offer

Our services.

Everything your car needs — from stance setups and bolt-on power to full audio builds and exterior installs. If it makes your car better, we do it.

Category 01

Wheels, tires & suspension.

Whether you're building a slammed show car or a precise track weapon, we dial in your suspension exactly how you want it.

Stance Setups

Dial in wheel offset, ride height, and camber to achieve aggressive stance without sacrificing driveability.

  • Wheel fitment consultation
  • Lowering spring & coilover installs
  • Camber kit installs
  • Flush & poke fitment

Street & Track Setup

Performance-oriented suspension tuning for drivers who want both street manners and track capability.

  • Coilover setup & corner balancing
  • Sway bar upgrades
  • Alignment for track or street
  • Bushing & subframe upgrades

Handling Upgrades & Diagnostics

Clunks, vague steering, or uneven wear? We diagnose and fix suspension issues and recommend upgrades that genuinely improve your driving experience.

  • Full suspension inspection
  • Ball joint & tie rod replacement
  • Strut & shock replacement
  • Steering & handling troubleshooting

Wheels & Tires

New set of wheels? We mount, balance, and set tire pressure properly, and advise on the best tire for your use case.

  • Wheel mounting & balancing
  • TPMS programming
  • Tire fitment advice
  • Spacer installs

Category 02

Bolt-on upgrades.

Real power gains without a full engine build. We install the bolt-ons that make a difference — intake to exhaust and everything between.

Cold Air Intakes

Increase airflow and get a better sound under the hood. Short ram and cold air intakes for most makes and models.

  • Cold air intake installs
  • Short ram intake installs
  • Intake spacers
  • Filter upgrades

Throttle Body Upgrades

A larger throttle body helps your engine breathe better at wide open throttle. We source and install the right one for your setup.

  • Throttle body swap
  • Throttle body spacers
  • Throttle body cleaning

Headers & Manifolds

Long-tube or shorty headers — proper header fitment makes a significant difference in power and exhaust tone.

  • Long-tube header installs
  • Shorty header installs
  • Exhaust manifold replacement
  • H-pipe & X-pipe installs

Cat-Back Exhaust

Get the sound and performance you want. Axle-back and cat-back systems installed with proper fitment and minimal drone.

  • Cat-back system installs
  • Axle-back installs
  • Muffler replacements
  • Active exhaust systems

Category 03

Audio installs.

From a simple head unit swap to a full custom audio build — we wire everything clean and make it sound right.

Head Unit Upgrades

Upgrade from your factory radio to a modern touchscreen unit with CarPlay, Android Auto, backup camera, and more.

  • Double-DIN & touchscreen installs
  • Apple CarPlay / Android Auto setup
  • Dash kit & wiring harness
  • Backup camera integration

Speaker Upgrades

Replace stock speakers with component or coaxial sets that actually sound good. Baffles, deadening, and tuning for best sound.

  • Component speaker sets
  • Coaxial speaker installs
  • Tweeters & crossovers
  • Speaker baffles & adapters

Subwoofer & Amp Installs

Clean power, proper wiring, and a box that fits your build. Full subwoofer and amplifier installs done right — no rat's nests.

  • Subwoofer & box installs
  • Amplifier wiring & install
  • Bass processor tuning
  • Custom enclosure solutions

Sound Deadening

Cut down road noise and vibration so you actually hear your music. Material applied to floors, doors, and trunk areas.

  • Door panel deadening
  • Floor & firewall treatment
  • Trunk deadening
  • Full vehicle treatments

Category 04

Exterior & cosmetics.

The finishing touches that make your build look as good as it performs. Exterior pieces installed cleanly with proper fitment.

Lighting Upgrades

Upgrade your headlights, taillights, fogs, and interior lighting for better visibility and a cleaner look.

  • LED headlight conversions
  • Taillight installs
  • Fog light upgrades
  • Interior LED upgrades

Aero & Body Kits

Front lips, side skirts, rear diffusers — properly test-fitted and installed for a clean, factory-style result.

  • Front lip & splitter installs
  • Side skirts
  • Rear diffusers
  • Full body kit installs

Spoilers & Decklids

Add a spoiler or decklid swap for a more aggressive look. Drilled, sealed, and fit properly so it looks factory.

  • Trunk spoiler installs
  • Decklid panel swaps
  • Wing installs
  • Gurney flap add-ons

Grille & Front-End

Aftermarket grille installs, fascia swaps, and front-end cosmetic work done clean and tight.

  • Grille replacements
  • Fascia swaps
  • Emblem & badge work
